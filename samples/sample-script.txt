INT. THRONE ROOM - DAY

HAROLD: I never thought I'd return here.

KING HOLDEN: Then why did you?

HAROLD: Because the kingdom needs its king.

KING HOLDEN: And what of the old wounds?

HAROLD: Some wounds never heal. Others we learn to carry.

Harold holds the silence. DISTANT THUNDER rumbles outside.

The room darkens. OMINOUS STRINGS SWELL.

INT. COURTYARD - NIGHT

KING HOLDEN
The council awaits your answer.

HAROLD
Tell them I need more time.

KING HOLDEN
Time is a luxury we no longer have.
